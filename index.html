About Oluwatobiloba

Hello! Welcome to my page!

I am Ogundele Oluwatobiloba Razaq, based in Lagos state. I am a student of Altschool of engineering with id number: ALT/SOE/024/1319 in the frontend engineering class.

I am a highly motivated and passionate individual driven to provide solution and innovate ways to serves people needs.

Currently, I am a Glazier in MM Glass Venture. I am an expert on glass:

Sales in crates and sizes
Cutting
Drilling
Edging
Tempering
Art works
Frosting
Installation e.g. shower cubicle, partitioning, frameless door, railings, etc.

I have five years of experience working in MM glass venture. During my time in MM glass venture I have:

  • Installed, repaired, and replace glass in windows, doors, and skylight.
  • Measured glass and other materials to determine the type and size of glass needed.
  • Cut and shape glass into needed specification using specialized tools.
  • Established relationship with new customers and develop existing relationships.
  • Ensured that all work met industry and company standards.
  • Provided customer service to customers while on job sites.
  • Completed work orders and provided written and verbal reports.
  • Adhere to safety protocols while working on job sites.
  • Maintained, repaired, and replace all tools of the trade.
  • Researched new techniques, materials, and tools for the trade.

If you are interested in speaking with me about glass installation, feel free to send me a message here.


In my downtime, I enjoy traveling, reading and listening to music. I am delighted to connect with new people.

Reason why I joined Altschool Africa

Observing the world in this digital age, I discovered that all profession must have their base in tech in order to reach the global world. This has prompted me to delve into the tech field. Choosing a niche was really hard for me because there were so many option. I went for frontend engineering because i heard it was quite tasking. There is a saying among the yorubas "where the going get tough you meet the men"

I was pondering on an institution to make my dream of becoming a software engineer come to fruition. I finally discuss it with my friend, a cyber securist. He then reccommended Altschool and other institutions. After accessing the institutions websites and social pages, I was impressed by the content of Altschool Africa. The duration and ease of learning at one's pace in combination with my work schedule excite me.

My goals for school of engineering program

To learn all about software engineering is my utmost desire. My ultimate goal is to utilize all I will be taught in Altschool of engineering to: become a renown software engineer, cooperate with fellow software engineer in tackling problems, reach the global world create a website for SME to promote their business.
